About Chillbert

Welcome to a space of calm and connection. I'm Chillbert, a licensed therapist specialising in eco-anxiety, mindfulness-based interventions, and sustainable living practices.

Living in the serene marshlands of Mistralshire has taught me that true healing comes from understanding our deep interconnection with the natural world. My practice integrates traditional therapeutic approaches with ecological wisdom, helping individuals navigate personal struggles while fostering a healthier relationship with our planet.

Whether you're dealing with climate anxiety, life transitions, or simply seeking greater mindfulness in your daily routine, my door is always open for compassionate, non-judgmental support.

Credentials & Training

  • Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW)
  • Certified Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction Instructor
  • Eco-Psychology Certificate, Mistralshire Institute
  • Specialisation in Climate Psychology
  • Wu Wei Philosophy & Applied Mindfulness

My Approach

Wu Wei - Effortless Action

Therapy doesn't have to be a struggle against yourself. By working with your natural rhythms and tendencies, we can create sustainable change that feels authentic and lasting.

One Health Perspective

Your wellbeing is interconnected with the health of your community and environment. True healing acknowledges these connections and works to strengthen all three dimensions.

Mindful Sustainability

Small, conscious changes compound over time. Whether in personal habits or environmental choices, mindful attention to our actions creates ripple effects of positive change.
